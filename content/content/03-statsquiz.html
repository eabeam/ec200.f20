What should I know?

Make sure you are comfortable with the material chapter guides from Week 2 and Week 3. What does comfortable mean? That you’re familiar with definitions and terminology, and that you can work through problems at the level of PS2 and PS3.

There will not be a Stata component to the exam.

What is the structure of the exam?

The exam will “go live” during our scheduled class time at 2:20pm on Monday, September 18. It will be open until Midnight. Once you start the exam, you will have 75 minutes to complete the exam (standard class duration + time for bathroom/craziness/deep breaths)

You will be asked to work through about 3-4 multi-part worth 30 points total. There are multiple versions of the exam.

How do I submit?

Work through on paper, then scan a copy and upload it onto Blackboard. Please use something like Adobe Scan so I don’t have a million .jpg files!
